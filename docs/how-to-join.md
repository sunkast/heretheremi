# How To Become A Member
Once you tap the join button on Facebook, you will be presented with a form that asks you a series of questions, and to confirm you have read the group rules. These questions help us maintain a safe, positive community and ensure the group guidelines are met.

1. Where in Michigan are you located, what age range best fits you, and your current relationship status?
2. What brings you here, and what kinds of in person events are you most interested in?
3. Have you read the group rules? Please include the keyword listed there in your answer.

## Ready To Join?
If you are ready to join a positive and welcoming community, we invite you to become a member.
